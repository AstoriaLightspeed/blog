Welcome to Astoria Lightspeed ISP Blog!

This website contains all the FAQ's and guides for Astoria Lightspeed ISP. If you have any questions, please contact us at contact@astorialightspeed.com

FAQ's

What is Astoria Lightspeed ISP?

Astoria Lightspeed ISP is a new ISP in Astoria, Oregon. We provide high speed internet to the Astoria area.

What is the difference between Astoria Lightspeed ISP and other ISP's?

Astoria Lightspeed ISP is a local ISP, meaning we are based in Astoria, Oregon. We are not a large corporation, and we are not a franchise. We are a local business, and we are here to help you. We also use a wireless and fiber mixture to provide internet to our customers.

How do I sign up for Astoria Lightspeed ISP?

You can sign up for Astoria Lightspeed ISP by going to our website at https://astorialightspeed.com and clicking on the "Contact Us" button. You will then be taken to a page where you can fill out a form to sign up for our service.

How do I pay for Astoria Lightspeed ISP?

You can pay for Astoria Lightspeed ISP by going to our website at https://panel.astorialightspeed.com and clicking on the "Pay Now" button. You will then be taken to a page where you can pay for your service.

How do I get a static IP address?

You can get a static IP address by emailing us at contact@astorialightspeed.com or calling us at (503) 000-0000. We will then give you a static IP address.

How do I get a domain name?

We recommend using Porkbun for domain names. They are a great company and they have great prices. You can also use Namecheap or GoDaddy if you want to.
